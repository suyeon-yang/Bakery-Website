Goals

"We are a small family-run local business, operating in Kirwan since 2012, hoping to improve our business by getting on the Internet. Homepage should be very appealing and drive interest in our bakery."

- The main goal of this web page is for expansion of their small family-run local bakery business by getting in the internet for further improvements. In addition, Sample images of the products should be visualized in pages to appeal and drive the interest of customers.

Success Evaluation

- The success can be evaluated by the increasing in the number of customers who will get attracted by the page and eventually sign up for weekly specials, and when more of young customers of age 17-35 come and visit their bakery.

Target Audience

"We have customers of all ages, however, we would like to attract young families and young people, possibly around the age of 17-35, to our bakery"

- The client wants young people for about 17 to 35 years old to be attracted to their web pages and eventually their bakery. To make this happen, designing in a modern and classy way could be a way to get attention from this specific age group.

Usability test plan

Goals and Objectives


  • The ultimate goal of the testing is to grasp feedback which will decide if modification of some aspects is necessary.
  • It is to understand how people navigate and look around the site - if it is being done similarly with the intention of the developer and the client.
  • It is to understand whether users are successfully doing what they are ought to do without difficulties in handling some attributes - such as finding elements and filling up the forms necessary.
  • It is to understand whether users can understand the information provided and if it is appropriate.

Targeting Users


  • The target users are people of 17-35 in particular as requested by the client.

Usability Test Report

Prior Questions

  1. What is first impression of the website?
  2. Can you explain your experience from navigating the pages? (you have to navigate based on the testers demand.
  3. Is it easy to use?
  4. Are there any errors? if so is error handling well described (test newsletterform)
  5. Does the website give feedback to the user?
  6. Does the user want to purchase and re-visit the site ?
  7. Any comments on user experience form the tester?

Learnability: How easy is it for users to accomplish basic tasks the first time they encounter the design?


- The users experienced a smooth exploration in navigating page to page. Necessary features and aspects were easy to find and was simple enough which avoided possible confusions. It was said that it seem perfectly planned overall, which made the website look prepared and tasks were able to be done without any doubts.


Efficiency: Once users have learned the design, how quickly can they perform tasks?


- The users proposed that the website was definitely easy to interact with. They expressed that there were no useless features that confused them and the form submitting section was also simple enough to interact with. Furthermore, they added that the validation of data were being done specifically in each portion which gave sufficient feedback when they input a wrong format of data.


Memorability: When users return to the design after a period of not using it, how easily can they reestablish proficiency?


- According to the feedbacks they gave, it can be inferred that the website would still be easy to handle even after a period of time. In addition, further developments may even enhance the quality of the website in upcoming future.


Errors: How many errors do users make, how severe are these errors, and how easily can they recover from the errors?


- The possible errors that the user may make in the website are when handling forms and its validating process. The errors are described as wrong and inappropriate inputs in the form. The users were able to recover from these errors for there were messages that served as their guidelines in completing the form successfully.


Satisfaction: How pleasant is it to use the design?


- They proposed that it was attractive and was aesthetically pleasing due to the good selection of color scheme for the website. They showed willingness to re-visit the website in the future as well. They added that the website was overall bright, eye-catching, and everything stayed in their proper positions and places.
